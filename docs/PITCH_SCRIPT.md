# CampusMate Pitch Script

## Opening

CampusMate is a calm, context-aware study assistant for university students who need to manage deadlines, study sessions and reminders without juggling several disconnected apps.

## Problem

Students often track assignments in notes, calendars and chat messages. This makes it easy to miss deadlines or forget work that depends on a specific campus location, such as studying in the library or preparing for a lab.

## Solution

CampusMate combines four workflows:

1. task and deadline management
2. location-aware reminders
3. focused study timing
4. weekly analytics with an accessible audio summary

## Key features to demonstrate

- Home dashboard with urgent task overview
- Task search, filters and detail actions
- Add/edit task form
- Map-based campus reminder
- Notification scheduling
- Study timer
- Analytics and text-to-speech summary
- Settings for theme, text size and accessibility

## Why it is useful

CampusMate is intentionally focused. It avoids the complexity of full project management tools while giving students enough structure to plan their week, act on deadlines and understand study progress.

## Future improvements

Future versions could sync with university calendars, support shared group tasks and provide smarter study recommendations based on location, deadlines and previous study sessions.
