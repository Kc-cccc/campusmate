# CampusMate Product Overview

CampusMate is a context-aware study assistant for university students. It combines task management, study timing, campus reminders and weekly analytics in one mobile product.

## Product problem

Students often manage deadlines across multiple subjects using scattered notes, calendar entries and messages. Important work can be missed because reminders are not connected to context such as time, place and study progress.

## Product solution

CampusMate gives students a single mobile workflow:

1. Add tasks with subject, priority and deadline.
2. Attach a campus location reminder when a task is linked to a place such as a library or lab.
3. Start a focused study session and save study minutes against the relevant subject.
4. Review weekly progress through simple charts and an audio summary.
5. Adjust theme, text size and accessibility preferences.

## Target users

- University students managing multiple subjects.
- First-year students who need a simple structure for deadlines.
- Students who study in different campus locations.
- Students who prefer accessible summaries and larger text.

## Product principles

- Clear navigation over complex feature depth.
- Calm visual design over noisy dashboard widgets.
- Local-first reliability with Firebase-ready sync.
- Visible feedback for every major action.
- Accessibility settings built into the product rather than added later.
